About Us

We are a young family of four looking to rent our house, fully stocked, while we are out of state caring for family.

Why Choose Us?

You cannot beat this beautiful two-story, 2200 sq. ft., 4 bedroom, 2 1/2 bath home. It comes fully stocked and ready for you and your family. Located in Clay, just 20 minutes from downtown Birmingham and less than 10 minutes from downtown Trussville. The master bedroom features two walk-in closets and an ensuite, private bathroom. The master bathtub comfortably fits two full-grown adults and features an entertainment system. Every bedroom and the living room come equipped with flatscreen TVs, Amazon Firesticks, and Amazon Alexas. The property has security cameras for which you can be assigned an access code for viewing at an additional cost. This home is fully secured with a Ring alarm system including motion sensors and contact sensors on all exterior access points. For an additional fee, you will be granted an arm/disarm code as well as the ability to customize home and away mode. All bedrooms are very spacious and located on the second floor. There is a fully stocked craft room for children on the main level. There is a girls bedroom with a bunk bed and a boys bedroom with a twin bed and a cot. Both children's bedrooms come fully stocked with toys suitable for ages 3-9. The backyard features a huge gorilla playset equipped with slides, a clubhouse, a sandbox, pirate spyglass, rock climbing wall, swings, rings, and swinging bar. The backyard also features a small heated pool, fireplace, grill, and marble tables. The kitchen comes fully equipped with top of the line appliances and every baking supply you can think of. For an additional fee, a neighboring chef can do your grocery shopping, give you recipes, and even cook breakfast for you!

Rental Information

The price to rent this home is $350 per night, plus all applicable taxes and fees. Taxes and fees are 33% and include our service fee, Alabama Lodging Tax, and Birmingham Lodging Tax. An additional cleaning fee of $250 will be charged to cover housekeeping services at the end of your stay. The full amount for the rental and any additional services selected is due at booking. After completing the contact form below, you will receive an invoice for the entire cost of your stay. After payment has been received, your dates will be blocked out in our calendar and you will be contacted with more information about the exact location of the property, as well as any additional check-in and check-out instructions. Normal check-in time is 10 AM, however, accommocations can be made. Normal check-out time is 12 PM, again, accommodations can be made.
